Sparse Mixture-of-Experts Routing for Long-Context Models

Authors: J. Okafor, R. Mehta, L. Persson
Affiliation: Institute for Scalable Computing

We study routing strategies for sparse mixture-of-experts (MoE) layers in
long-context transformers, showing that load-balanced top-k routing preserves
quality while cutting active parameters by 60%. Our analysis builds on prior
work in efficient attention and conditional computation.

Annual Sustainability Report 2023
GreenGrid Energy Cooperative

Published March 2024 by the Office of Corporate Responsibility.
Contact: sustainability@greengrid.coop  |  +1 (415) 555-0148

Executive Summary
In 2023, GreenGrid Energy Cooperative reduced operational carbon emissions by
18% relative to its 2021 baseline, driven by a transition to solar-plus-storage
microgrids across twelve service regions. Member-owned generation capacity grew
to 240 megawatts, and the cooperative reinvested 31% of net surplus into
community resilience programs. This report details progress against the
cooperative's 2030 net-zero commitments, governance changes adopted by the
member assembly, and the methodology used to audit emissions.

Sections: Governance, Emissions Accounting, Community Programs, 2030 Roadmap.

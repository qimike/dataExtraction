Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Clinical Question Answering
Patel, A., Nguyen, T., and Rossi, M. (2024)
Proceedings of the Conference on Health Informatics, vol. 11, pp. 88-104.

Corresponding author: a.patel@meditech-research.org
DOI: 10.1109/HCI.2024.0042

Abstract
We present a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system for answering
clinical questions over electronic health records. Building on the work of
Lewis et al. (2020) and the dense-retrieval methods of Karpukhin et al.
(2020), our approach grounds each generated answer in retrieved passages and
reports calibrated uncertainty. Across three benchmark datasets, the system
improves answer accuracy by 9.4% over a strong non-retrieval baseline while
reducing unsupported claims, as discussed by Patel et al. (2024).

Keywords: retrieval-augmented generation, clinical NLP, question answering
